The Hole

A virtual puppet play about escaping from despair

Instructions

Enable your webcam and use your hands to control the puppet's movements.

To open the puppet's mouth, make a fist. To close the mouth, open your hand.

Use the buttons below or their corresponding keys to change the location and play sound effects.

Press the mute button or hit Key M to toggle all audio on or off.

Location

Fields [Key Q]
Field with Hole [Key A]
Hole [Key W]
Hole with Rope [Key S]

Sound Effects

Thud [Key Z]
Rope Dropping [Key X]
Footsteps [Key C]

Sound Control

Mute All [Key M]
